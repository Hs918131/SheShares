How We Work

About Us

"Sheshares is a girls empowering girls based website that motivates women of all ages to share their issues with other women around the globe and get their problems resolved in an efficient manner which at the end leads to the development of self esteem, mentorship skills and recognise the value of community service"

Our Goal

Build Solidarity

We're stronger together. Discover resources and solutions related to the issues, regions, and topics you care about most.

Share Stories

No one speaks for you; you speak for yourself. Post original stories to inspire and inform tens of thousands of women who support gender equity

Make Connection

Connect deeply with women who are making a difference in their communities- no mater how big or small.

What makes us different?

We are Community-Led

We start by listening - communities identify their own challenges and we partner with them to plan and achieve long-term, tailored solutions

We are Girl-Focused

In all we do, we work towards girls’ complete wellbeing, so they can grow up with hope and realise their potential.

We are Privacy-Based

Sheshares guarantees that your identity is not revealed and ensures best solution to your problem.
